Lasagna Recipe

Our delicious homemade lasagna recipe!

Here we bring you our traditional homemade lasagna recipe. Please remember to use the freshest ingriedients, and enjoy!

ingriedients

  • lasagna sheet noodles
  • mozarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • butter
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • tomato sauce
  • ground beef
  1. Add a little water to the ground beef, eggs, bread crumbs, and romano in a bowl. Mix with your hands.
  2. Add the meat mixture to a heated, oiled skillet and break up the filling with a spoon.
  3. Brown the meat until it's no longer pink. If you see any excess fat, drain it.
  4. Add a couple layers of sauce, then lay down some lasagna noodles in a single layer. Spread ricotta on the pasta.
  5. Add a third of the meat filling on top of the ricotta.
  6. Sprinkle on a third of the shredded mozzarella.
  7. Sprinkle on some parmesan to cover the layer.
  8. Add one last layer of pasta, in the opposite direction as the previous layer.
  9. Sprinkle on some parmesan and ladle on some sauce.
  10. Now that your tradtional Italian lasagna is fully assembled, you can cover it with greased foil and bake, or refrigerate it to bake the following day.
  11. when ready to bake, preheat oven to 350 F, and bake for 30 minutes.
